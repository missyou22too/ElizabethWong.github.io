Hi, I'm Elizabeth!
Transforming raw information into insight, clarity, and direction.

I'm a data enthusiast who loves uncovering insights hidden within complex datasets. My journey from cancer research to data science has taught me that behind every number is a pattern waiting to be discovered. As our world becomes increasingly data drive and information dense it is imperative that also adapt to the new landscape around us. Data is all around us, and its our job to use that opportunity.

Doing What I Love

I began my career in cancer health care as a Research Technician at the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center, where I had the opportunity to develop and lead new immunology studies. I loved following our experiments from beginning to end and striving for high quality results to provide the best care to our patients.

The more time I spent working, the more I was drawn to the data analysis side of research. I was absolutely blown away by the amount of data we generate in every study, and how important it is to understand the underlying story that data tells us. From there, I began teaching myself to code and eventually completed MIT's Applied Data Science Program to spearhead my career pivot.


Currently I work as a Data & Insight Specialist with Regatta-Playbook, a sailing analysis startup that plans to revolutionize the current field of data-driven raceboat analytics.
